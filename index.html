How Does This Work?

In simple terms, carpooling/bike pooling/ride-sharing refers to sharing empty seats in a vehicle. People commuting from one point to another can share empty seats with other passengers who are travelling on the same route.

Download The App

Request A Ride

We can get you there. Simply request a ryde on the app and we'll connect you with a cab immediately.

Pick A Price

Pick the valuation you're willing to pay and your ryde is set up. The only surge is the acceleration you get when taking off.

Ride Off To Success!

After matching with cab and agreeing to its terms, you’ll be all set. Your cab will arrive shortly to pick you up.
